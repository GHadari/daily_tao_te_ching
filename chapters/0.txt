If you can talk about it,
it ain't Tao.
If it has a name,
it's just another thing.

Tao doesn't have a name.
Names are for ordinary things.

Stop wanting stuff; 
it keeps you from seeing what's real.
When you want stuff, 
all you see are things.

Those two sentences 
mean the same thing.
Figure them out, 
and you've got it made.
